Frequently Asked Questions

These answers cover delivery, orders, products and account support.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are dispatched within 24 hours and usually arrive in 2 to 3 business days, depending on your location.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes. HerbAtlas offers free shipping on orders above Rs.999. Eligible carts automatically show free shipping at checkout.

How do I use the HERB10 coupon?

Enter HERB10 in the coupon field on the cart or checkout page. If the coupon is valid for your order, 10% off is applied instantly.

Can I track my order after placing it?

Yes. Open the My Orders page from your account menu to review your latest orders, totals and order status.

Are HerbAtlas products 100% organic?

HerbAtlas products are listed as organic and safety-labelled wherever applicable. Product detail pages and shop cards show the current label clearly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, net banking and cash on delivery for orders above Rs 500.

Can I return a product?

We do not accept returns because herb powder products are consumable. If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, contact us within 48 hours and include a photo of the package.

How should I store herb powders?

Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep the pouch sealed after each use and try to consume the product within 6 months of opening.

Are your herbs safe for children?

Most herbs are not recommended for children under 12. Please consult a pediatrician before giving any herbal supplement to a child.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently we deliver only within India. International shipping is not available yet.

How do I know which herb is right for me?

Visit the Ailments page and use the HerbAtlas recommender flow to explore herbs based on your concern, safety notes and intended use.

Do these herbs replace medical treatment?

No. HerbAtlas content and products are provided for educational and wellness purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before using herbal supplements.
